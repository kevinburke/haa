# HAA Timeline

September 2016: Los Gatos [rejects proposed North 40 plan by a 3-2 vote][north-40-rejected].

[north-40-rejected]: http://www.mercurynews.com/2016/09/02/los-gatos-town-council-rejects-proposed-north-40-development/

March 2017: [arguments heard][north-40-arguments] in North 40 case.

[north-40-arguments]: http://www.mercurynews.com/2017/04/07/the-north-40-has-its-day-in-court/

June 2017: CA State Senate passes SB 167, strengthening
the Housing Accountability Act. Text of SB 167: [here][sb-167]

[sb-167]: https://leginfo.legislature.ca.gov/faces/billCompareClient.xhtml?bill_id=201720180SB167

June 2017: Judge [rules against Los Gatos][los-gatos-order], orders them to
reconsider decision to block North 40 development.

[los-gatos-order]: http://www.mercurynews.com/2017/06/14/judge-rules-against-town-in-north-40-lawsuit/
